Master the Art of Connection

Based on Dale Carnegie's "How to Win Friends and Influence People" & Chris Voss's "Never Split the Difference"

1 The Mirroring Method

Mirror the customer's pace, tone, and key words to build unconscious rapport.

  • 1 Listen for their speaking pace (fast/slow)
  • 2 Match their energy level (calm/excited)
  • 3 Repeat their exact key phrases back
  • 4 Use similar vocabulary complexity

2 The Name Game

A person's name is the sweetest sound to them - use it strategically.

  • 1 Use their name within first 30 seconds
  • 2 Repeat it 2-3 times during the call
  • 3 End with their name for lasting impression

3 The Tactical Empathy

From "Never Split the Difference" - Acknowledge emotions before they're expressed.

  • 1 "It sounds like this has been frustrating..."
  • 2 "You probably feel like we haven't listened..."
  • 3 "I can hear you're concerned about..."
